SmartCV

“Bridge the gap between education and career.”

1. Startup Core Idea

SmartCV is an intelligent ecosystem designed for final-year students and fresh graduates (0-3 years experience). It focuses on enhancing candidate quality through a "Learn – Do – Share" model.

  • Mission: To provide students with a comprehensive, reputable toolkit to master their first professional recruitment journey.
  • Vision: To become the leading career preparation platform for the Vietnamese youth economy.
  • Problem Solved: Addresses the lack of industry-specific CV guidance, robotic interview prep tools, and the absence of verified company culture insights for entry-level roles.

3. Target Value to Customer

SmartCV offers tiered access to ensure every student can start their journey, regardless of their budget.

Feature Pillar Detail Offering Core Value Benefit
Interview Training Video series on psychology & body language + Question Bank Builds confidence and reduces anxiety for first-time interviewees.
Verified Reviews Reviews verified via Student ID/Internship Contracts Provides transparent, trustworthy insights into work environments.
Community Hub Personalized feedback from HR experts & Peer sharing Turns the lonely job-hunting process into a collaborative experience.

4. Why SmartCV Stands Out?

  • High Trust Mechanism: Unlike general review sites, our reviews require verification (Student ID/Invoices), solving the problem of fake feedback.
  • Industry Verticality: Guides are not generic; they are tailored specifically for Design, IT, Marketing, and Finance.
  • Integrated Ecosystem: We move the user from passive learning (watching videos) to active practice (question banks) and finally to real-world sharing (blog).
